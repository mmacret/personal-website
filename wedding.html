You are about to discover the land of Matthieu's ancestors! The Somme is located in the north of rural France, a 2 hour drive from Paris.

Here is an overview of places to visit and things to do!

Corbie

Corbie is Matthieu's birthplace and where the wedding ceremony will take place. It is a small town of 6000 inhabitants. Don't miss the abbey church and the city hall where the religious ceremony and the civil ceremony will take place.

Website

Amiens

Amiens is the closest large city by Corbie (20 min drive). You should definitely visit the Amiens Cathedral that makes Notre Dame de Paris looks like a small church. Don't miss Saint-Leu quarter just behind the cathedral. It is the perfect place to go for a stroll and get a drink.

If you feel like going on a barge tour, you can visit "floating gardens" called Hortillonnages.

Website

Baie de Somme

The Baie de Somme is the west coast of the Somme. You can go for a bike ride by Le Crotoy, see the cliffs next to Ault, birdwatch at Marquenterre park or go for a stroll by the sea at Saint Valery sur Somme. If you are lucky, you can also see seals!

Website

WWI Battlefields

The Battle of the Somme was a battle of WWI fought by the armies of the British and French empires against the German Empire. It was one of the largest battles of WWI, in which more than 1,000,000 men were wounded or killed, making it one of the bloodiest battles in human history. In you are interested, you can still see the remains of the tranches and there is a lot of museums, monuments, and cemeteries to visit.

Website

Abbeville

Abbeville is the closest large town to the castle where the reception will take place. Do not miss the Cathedral of St. Vulfran with its super nice facade!
