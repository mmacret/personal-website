Hello, I'm Matthieu Macret, a Software Engineer, Artificial Intelligence Researcher and recent M.Sc. Graduate from SIAT - SFU currently working at the Metacreation Lab in Vancouver.

As a research assistant at the Metacreation Lab, I investigate the potential of Evolutionary Computation to help sound designers, musicians or composers to automatically generate new sound synthesis engines or further explore existing ones. I design, implement, test and evaluate new prototypes and present the results in international conferences and journals.
